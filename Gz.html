About Adjira

Classic Study Planner was designed to bridge the gap between modern productivity tools and the timeless elegance of traditional pen-and-paper planning. We believe that a calm, organized environment is the key to effective learning.

Philosophy

We focus on distraction-free interfaces. No ads, no notifications, just you and your goals. Whether you are studying for university, learning a new language, or reading the books, this tool adapts to your rhythm.
